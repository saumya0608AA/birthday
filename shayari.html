For you my Dumbo

Teri dakhal se khawabon ko haseen karne laga hun main,
Tujhe dekhkar apsaron me yakeen karne laga hun main.
Tere zulfe leharaye bas isliye hawa bani hai,
Tere aashikon ke liye pagalpan ki dawa bani hai.
Tu shayaron ko ghazal deti hai, Santo ko badal deti hai,
Zamana teri or aata hai, Tu muh fer kar chal deti hai.
